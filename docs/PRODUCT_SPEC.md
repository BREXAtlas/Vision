# Product Specification

Vision 2031 helps Lawrence practice a five-year identity through daily evidence. The first experience is a possible future day, followed by a reverse reveal of the systems beneath it. Forward play combines narrative, decisions, sourced lessons, quizzes, journaling, visualization, and bridge actions. Modes support today, catch-up, replay, and author preview without shame mechanics.

Success means consistent evidence across vision, knowledge, execution, revenue, research, leadership, wellbeing, and legacy—not guaranteed wealth. Game indicators never represent actual financial performance.
