# Story Bible

The narrator is first-person Lawrence. Future scenes are explicitly aspirational and editable. Family members remain narrative roles; TOS remains undefined. Luxury illustrates time leverage and stewardship only after productive assets, governance, people, and risk are visible. Staff are skilled professionals, never props. The emotional arc moves from spectacle to systems, ownership, evidence, peace, and responsibility.

The final truth: “The board was never about objects. It was about becoming the person capable of stewarding the life.”
